The Mission Briefing

Started in 1951 by the National Society of Professional Engineers, Engineer's Week highlights engineers' contributions for social good on the second week of February. E-Week is observed by a plethora of professional and academic societies as well as corporations and government agencies.

At UIUC, E-Week is celebrated through a friendly competition, where over 300 students and over 40 societies annually participate. 5 or 10 man teams engage in events and challenges for points, competing for the E-Week Champion title at the end of the week. E-Week's activities center around a different theme every year. This year, the theme is Outer Space.
