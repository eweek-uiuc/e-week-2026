Refueling // Lunch

When is the deadline to register?

The hard deadline is 2/19, 11:59 PM. This deadline ensures that all team leaders can attend a leader's meeting before the competition week begins.

Logistics // Points

How does my team earn points?

Points are awarded for event attendance, competition wins, and participating in the daily society challenges.

Logistics // Team Size

How many people can be in a team?

The amount of people you can have on a team depends on if you plan to be a 10-person or 5-person team (each event has an option for either size!). 5-person teams are allowed 5 players, with a 6th person to be used as an alternate. 10-person teams are allowed 10 players, with 2 more to be used as alternates.
